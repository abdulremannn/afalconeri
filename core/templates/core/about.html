About AFalconeri

A defense technology company built on a conviction that sovereignty requires technological superiority, not dependency.

Company Vision

The AFalconeri
Doctrine

AFalconeri Technologies was established with a singular purpose: to develop advanced aerospace and defense systems that provide strategic and tactical superiority to those who defend sovereign territory and democratic institutions.

We operate at the intersection of aerospace engineering, artificial intelligence, and defense electronics — developing platforms that are not incremental improvements on existing designs, but genuine step-changes in operational capability.

Our founding team brings together expertise from military aviation, advanced manufacturing, AI research, and signals intelligence. We understand the operational environment because we have operated within it, and we build technology that reflects the reality of modern conflict.

We are not a traditional defense contractor. We are a technology-first organization that applies the velocity and innovation culture of a high-performance engineering company to the uncompromising standards of defense systems development. That combination — speed, rigor, and operational understanding — is our competitive advantage.

Development History

Company Timeline

From initial concept to operational systems. AFalconeri Technologies has moved rapidly from research through prototype to active platform deployment.

2022 — FOUNDING
Company Established
AFalconeri Technologies incorporated. Initial R&D team assembled from aerospace and defense backgrounds. Concept studies initiated for surveillance platform architecture.
2023 — PROTOTYPE
Falcon X1 First Flight
Falcon X1 prototype completes maiden flight. EO/IR payload integration validated. 12-hour endurance achieved in initial test configuration. Encrypted datalink performance confirmed.
2023 — DEVELOPMENT
Falcon X2 Design Finalized
Tactical UAV airframe design finalized following extensive CFD optimization. Modular payload architecture approved. Manufacturing partner contracts initiated.
2024 — OPERATIONAL
Falcon X1 Operational Clearance
Falcon X1 achieves operational status following extended validation program. 18-hour endurance record established. First partner procurement contracts signed.
2024 — PROTOTYPE
Falcon L3 Development Begins
Loitering munition program initiated. AI targeting subsystem development commenced. Tube-launch configuration validated in static testing.
ONGOING — CLASSIFIED
Advanced Programs
Multiple classified development programs active across advanced strike systems, electronic warfare, and autonomous swarm architectures. Details available under NDA.
